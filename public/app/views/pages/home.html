About

The Clan

Established on November 1st, 2015, Space Monkeys is a brand new fair-play clan dedicated to wars, winning, and improving together. When fully functional, clan wars will consist of town hall levels 8-10 with varying strategies that give the best opportunities for 3 stars. We are not an elite level clan, but we aim for this clan to do so in the near future.

The Leaders

The leaders of this clan are experienced war veterans that are dedicated to improving individually as well as a team. We can offer advice at any town hall level and will help in any way that we can. Recently coming from a highly skilled war clan that just met some inactivity, we are ready to lead and win wars ultimately!
